Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Mix the shredded chicken, cream cheese, hot sauce, ranch and 1/2 of the cheddar cheese in a large bowl.
  3. Scoop mixture into a baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
  4. Bake 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through.
  5. Serve with chips, crackers, bread, or veggies.
