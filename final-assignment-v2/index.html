Hi! I'm Rachel Neal, and I am an environmental communicator with a knack for using multimedia to convey complex ideas. I am passionate about environmental literacy as a tool to form more equitable societies and address our most pressing problems. In my work, I hope to reduce the knowledge gap between scientists and the public to empower people to make more informed decisions in their communities.

Outside of the professional realm, you can likely find me dabbling in arts and crafts, fly fishing, or playing with my dog, Appa.
